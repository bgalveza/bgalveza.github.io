Short bio

I am a third-year PhD student under the supervision of David Chataur at LAMFA (Laboratoire Amiénois de Mathématique Fondamentale et Appliquée) and Jérôme Scherer at EPFL. Prior to my doctoral studies, I was a student at École Polytechnique, where I also obtained a Master’s degree in Pure Mathematics from Sorbonne Université. My academic journey began at the University of Chile, where I did part of my undergraduate studies.
